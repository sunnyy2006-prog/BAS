About Us

Building Tomorrow's Leaders Today

Bright Angels School is a premier educational institution located in the heart of Madhepura, Bihar. We provide quality education from Nursery to Class 10, nurturing young minds with a perfect blend of academics, co-curricular activities, and moral values.

Our dedicated team of experienced teachers ensures every student receives individual attention and the best possible education in a safe and supportive environment. We follow the latest curriculum with modern teaching methodologies.

Meet Our Director

School Director

Niku Niraj

Director & Principal

With exceptional leadership and a deep passion for education, Mr. Niku Niraj has been the driving force behind Bright Angels School's success. Under his visionary guidance, the school has grown into a trusted institution in Madhepura, Bihar, known for academic excellence and holistic development.

He believes in creating an environment where every child can discover their unique potential and shine bright in all walks of life.

"Every child is a bright angle of our future. Our mission is to help them find their direction and illuminate the world."
